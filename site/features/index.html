Features

Every step of the grant lifecycle, in one place.

From the first scan of a funder database to the moment your application is received, AutoGrantHunter is built to remove the friction researchers shouldn't be paying for.

Discovery

  • Continuous scan of 12,000+ federal, foundation, and corporate funders
  • Daily digest tuned to your topic, eligibility, and career stage
  • Historical award data on every opportunity

Match scoring

  • Fit score based on your CV, prior awards, and project brief
  • Pre-flight eligibility check before you invest in writing
  • Reviewer-pool intelligence for select sponsors

Drafting

  • Specific Aims, Significance, Approach, Broader Impact, in your voice
  • Budget justification grounded in your institution's rates
  • Figures-aware editing with caption support

Compliance

  • Sponsor-specific format checks (page limits, fonts, margins)
  • Required-section coverage with red-team review
  • Biosketch and Other Support automation (NIH, NSF)

Collaboration

  • Real-time co-edit with PI, budget officer, and program staff
  • Comment threading with sponsor-aware suggestions
  • Role-based permissions and audit log

Knowledge base

  • Private vault of your prior proposals and publications
  • Reusable sections with auto-update on new awards
  • Citation library synced with Zotero and EndNote

Workflow

  • Deadline dashboard across every active application
  • Internal review routing with stage gates
  • Submission packager for ASSIST, Workspace, and ProposalCentral

Outcomes

  • Win-rate dashboards by funder, program, and team member
  • Reviewer comment ingestion for resubmissions
  • Portfolio-level forecasting and capacity planning
