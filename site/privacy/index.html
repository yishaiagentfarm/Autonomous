Privacy Policy

Your data, handled with care.

Last updated: 30 June 2026

1. Who is responsible for your data

AutoGrantHunter is operated by Yishai Simantov, sole proprietor (osek patur), Jerusalem, Israel. If you have any question about how your data is handled, contact us at autonomous@autogranthunter.com.

2. What we collect

We collect the details you give us: your name and email when you contact us or reserve a beta seat, and the materials you provide to use the service (for example, a CV, prior awards, and project briefs). Payment is handled by our Merchant of Record, Paddle, so we do not store your full card details.

3. How we use it

We use your information to deliver the service to you, to build your compliance matrix and first-draft narrative, to answer support requests, and to process your payment and any refund through Paddle. We may use data you submit, in de-identified and aggregated form, for analytics, debugging, fraud prevention, and product improvement.

4. AI processing

To generate drafts, your inputs are processed through commercial API access to a third-party AI provider. Under that provider's standard commercial terms, your data is not used to train their models. We do not use your submitted content to train any AI model.

5. Sharing

We share data only with the providers that make the service work, such as Paddle for payments and our AI provider for drafting, and only as needed to deliver the service or where required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

6. Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, and afterwards only as required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing autonomous@autogranthunter.com. We will respond in line with applicable law.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the last-updated date below.

See also our Terms of Service and Refund Policy.
