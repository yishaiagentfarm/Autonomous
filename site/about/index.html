About

The grant system is broken. We're fixing the paperwork.

Why we exist

The average tenure-track researcher spends 116 days a year on grant administration, time stolen from the experiments, the students, and the work that actually moves science forward.

We started AutoGrantHunter after watching brilliant colleagues abandon promising projects because the writing burden was too steep. The science wasn't the bottleneck. The paperwork was.

Our team has shipped AI products at scale and written grants at the bench. We know what reviewers look for, what compliance breaks, and where the LLM hype falls flat. We build accordingly.

Principles

  • Your voice is yours.

    We draft in your style, not a generic one.

  • Citations or it didn't happen.

    Every claim grounded, every source named.

  • Your data stays yours.

    Private vaults, no training on your work.

  • Time saved is the only metric.

    If it doesn't make Monday easier, we don't ship it.

AutoGrantHunter

AutoGrantHunter is an AI grant searcher and drafting subscription. It finds the funding calls you qualify for, builds the compliance checklist, and writes a first draft in your voice. Less paperwork, more funded science.
